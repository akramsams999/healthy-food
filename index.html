Steam beef with Veg. Salad

Cut beef into ½-inch thick steaks and place in bowl. In a small bowl stir together the soy sauce, bouillon, pepper, onion powder, paprika and then divide mixture in half and reserve half for the end of the recipe. Brush half of the mixture onto the steaks, thoroughly coating each side. Allow meat to stand for 30 minutes. Then eat this dish with Salad

Peas with onion and yogurt

You’ll blanch the pearl onions in hot water for 5 minutes. Then saute them in butter for 2 minutes. Add the peas and cook 1 more minute. Next add 1/4 cup of flour and cook and stir for 1 minute to cook the flour and get rid of the raw flour flavor. Stir in 1/2 cup of chicken broth and 1/2 cup of heavy cream. Add 1 teaspoon each of salt and sugar and 1/2 teaspoon of pepper. Cook just long enough to thicken the sauce and it’s ready to serve.
